## 🎯 WHO THIS IS FOR

- ✅ Bettors tired of chasing wins and ready to chase **Closing Line Value (CLV)**
- ✅ People who understand **prediction is entertainment, pricing is edge**
- ✅ Anyone willing to follow a system through variance with discipline
- ✅ Those who want to treat sports betting like a **market**, not a lottery

## 🚫 WHO THIS IS NOT FOR

- ❌ Parlay chasers and lottery ticket buyers
- ❌ People looking for "locks" or guaranteed winners
- ❌ Bettors who can't handle 20-game losing streaks
- ❌ Anyone unwilling to track every single wager
